The largest export-oriented high-tech wooden toy factory in China*

Lucy&Leo factory was founded in 2012. Engaged in serial production of products, is a linear production of a full cycle. Which includes: milling, grinding, laser cutting, 3D printing, sewing, thermal transfer, silk screen printing, UV printing, powder coating, wire bending, quality control, packaging, R&D research, prototyping and industrial designing.

The factory produces more than 100 types of innovative educational toys under the Lucy&Leo brand, sets for kindergartens and schools. Provides services for the development and contract manufacturing of products.

*In 2021, the factory was ranked among the top ten exporters of wooden toys in China by Zhejiang Province, Yunhe County, where a cluster of wooden products factories is based.
